FAQ

Common Questions

Do you build native or cross-platform apps?+
We build native Android apps (Kotlin) for maximum performance and full platform integration. We also offer React Native cross-platform development if you need both Android and iOS from a single codebase.
How long does an Android app take to build?+
Simple apps with 5–8 screens typically take 4–8 weeks. Feature-rich apps with backend integration, payments, or real-time features usually take 3–5 months.
Will you also build the backend/server?+
Yes. We offer full-stack development including backend APIs, databases, and admin panels alongside your Android app. Or we can integrate with an existing API you already have.
Do I need a Google Play developer account?+
Yes — a Google Play developer account is required to publish apps and costs $25 (one-time). We'll walk you through creating one and handle the entire submission process on your behalf.
