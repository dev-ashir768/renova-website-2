Our Process

How We Build Your Brand

1

Brand Discovery

We learn about your business, audience, values, competitors, and the feeling you want your brand to evoke. This informs every design decision.

2

Concept Development

We explore multiple creative directions and present 3–5 distinct logo concepts with rationale for each approach.

3

Refinement

You choose your preferred direction and we refine it until it's exactly right. Revisions are included — we don't stop until you love it.

4

Brand System Build-Out

We extend your logo into a full brand system — colors, typography, supporting graphics, and all collateral materials.

5

Delivery

All files delivered in every format you need (AI, EPS, SVG, PNG, PDF) plus a complete brand style guide.

FAQ

Common Questions

How many logo concepts do I get?+
We present 3–5 distinct initial concepts. After you select your preferred direction, we refine it through multiple revision rounds until you're completely satisfied.
What file formats do I receive?+
You receive all industry-standard formats: Adobe Illustrator (AI), EPS, SVG (scalable vector), PNG (transparent background), and PDF. Both full-color and black-and-white versions.
Do I own the final designs?+
Yes — once paid, you own full copyright to all final approved designs. You're free to use them anywhere, anytime, without restrictions.
