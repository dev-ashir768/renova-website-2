FAQ

Common Questions

Do you build for iPhone only or iPad too?+
We build for both iPhone and iPad. We'll discuss your target audience and build a universal app or a device-specific experience depending on your use case.
How long does App Store review take?+
Apple's review process typically takes 24–72 hours for new apps. We prepare your submission carefully to minimize rejection risk and handle any reviewer feedback promptly.
Do I need an Apple Developer account?+
Yes — an Apple Developer Program account is required to publish on the App Store. It costs $99/year. We'll help you set it up and guide you through enrolling your business account.
Can you also build the Android version?+
Yes. We offer both native Android (Kotlin) and cross-platform (React Native) development. If you need both iOS and Android, a React Native approach can significantly reduce cost and time.
