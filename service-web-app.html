Our Process

How We Build Your Application

1

Requirements & Architecture

We map out every feature, user role, data flow, and integration before writing code. This prevents costly changes mid-build and ensures the final product matches your vision.

FAQ

Common Questions

What technologies do you use?+
We primarily work with React, Next.js, Node.js, Laravel, and PostgreSQL/MySQL. We'll recommend the best stack based on your project's requirements, scale, and long-term needs.
How long does a web application take to build?+
Simple apps with 5–10 features typically take 4–8 weeks. More complex platforms with advanced features, integrations, or multi-user roles can take 3–6 months. We'll give you a detailed timeline estimate after your requirements call.
Will I own the source code?+
Yes. Once the project is fully paid and delivered, you own 100% of the source code. We can also transfer it to your own repository upon request.
Can you work with an existing codebase?+
Yes. We can audit, extend, and improve existing applications. We'll review your current code, identify technical debt, and propose the best path forward.
