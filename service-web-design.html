FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a website take to build?+
Most websites are completed in 5–14 business days depending on complexity. Simple landing pages can be done in as little as 3–5 days, while multi-page e-commerce sites typically take 10–20 days.
Do you build websites on WordPress or custom code?+
We work with both — WordPress/CMS for content-heavy sites that need easy self-management, and custom-coded solutions for maximum performance and full design freedom. We'll recommend the best approach for your project.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?+
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across mobile, tablet, and desktop. With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile, this is non-negotiable for us.
Do you offer hosting and domain setup?+
Yes. We can assist with domain registration, hosting setup, and SSL certificates. We work with all major hosting providers and can recommend the best option for your budget and traffic needs.
What do you need from me to get started?+
We'll need your brand assets (logo, colors, fonts if available), content (text and images, or we can help with that), and examples of websites you like. We'll guide you through the rest in our kickoff call.
