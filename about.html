Our Story

Built to Help Businesses Thrive

Renova Marketing Solutions was founded with a single purpose: to bridge the gap between great businesses and the audiences they deserve to reach.

We saw too many talented entrepreneurs and business owners being held back by fragmented, unreliable, or overpriced marketing support. So we built a full-service agency that does it all — from building your website to managing your brand's digital presence, running influencer campaigns, and even placing ads on digital billboards across North America.

Today, we serve clients across the United States and Canada, combining a creative team with technical expertise and a results-first mindset. Every project we take on gets the same level of care, strategy, and execution — whether it's a local startup or an established corporation.
